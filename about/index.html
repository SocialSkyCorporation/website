About

 

Micro is a globally distributed technology company on a mission to simplify the way we build, share and collaborate on services in the Cloud and beyond. We do this with a developer first focus.


Overview

The world is moving towards becoming entirely driven by technology. Every business must now embrace the idea of becoming a technology company. Making this change is hard. Maintaining a competitive edge in this day and age is becoming increasingly difficult.

With the emergence of the Cloud we've accelerated software delivery and unlocked elasticity in infrastructure management through APIs but software development tools have lagged behind. At Micro we've developed a model for effectively leveraging the Cloud and now want to share that with the world.


Design

Microservices is a software design pattern which accelerates development by breaking down applications into a number of smaller services which can be developed, deployed and scaled independently.

The concept of microservices is not new, it's the reimagining of service oriented architecture (SOA). An approach more holistically aligned with the unix philosophy of small individual processes that do one thing well, which can then be combined via their inputs and outputs.


The way we imagine microservices today is as follows:

  • Do one thing well - Services follow the unix philosophy and focus on solving one problem

  • Automate everything - Automation, fast iteration and testing need to be part of the system

  • Embrace failure - The culture and design principles should follow anti-fragility

Architecture

A microservices architecture enables products to be broken down into smaller services which teams work on and scale independently of each other. Each team became responsible for the delivery of their particular service which others came to use through common APIs.

Here are just a few of the benefits:

  • 1. Easier to scale - teams organise around different business requirements and manage their own services.
  • 2. Reduced cognitive load - microservices are much smaller, usually 1000 LOC or less.
  • 3. Improved fault tolerance - separation of concerns minimises the impact of issues in one service from another.
  • 4. Speed of execution - develop, deploy and manage microservices independently.
  • 5. Rapid prototyping and reuse - a microservices philosophy promotes reuse and accelerated development.

Technology

Micro is a development platform for cloud services. We provide a Runtime and Framework for developers to build, run and manage Micro services. We are the defacto standard for cloud services development and will look to empower companies to leverage this technology for their own businesses. From early stage prototyping all the way through to large scale production deployments.

Developers need to be given the tools to innovate and rapidly deliver services to end users without having to worry about infrastructure concerns. Micro is doing this through the delivery of a development platform for cloud services.
